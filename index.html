Hex #16BBC3

These colors can be easily changed but it is important to remember to be specific when naming classes.

Fonts

Tajawal

  • Light 300
  • Regular 400
  • Bold 700

Quatrocento

  • Regular 400
  • Bold 700

Tajawal is a sans-serif font while Quatrocento is a serif-font; meaning one has the little extra lines on the letters and the other does not (sans).

Text Styles

H1: Main heading

  1. Font size: 28px
  2. Font weight: Bold
  3. Font family: Quatrocento

H2: Subheading

  1. Font size: 18px
  2. Font weight: Regular
  3. Font family: Quatrocento

P: paragraph

  1. Font size: 15px
  2. Font weight: Regular
  3. Font family: Quatrocento
